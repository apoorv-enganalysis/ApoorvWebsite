About Me

I am a passionate mechanical engineer with 10 years of industry experience, currently working at ASML where I contribute to the development of cutting-edge semiconductor manufacturing equipment. My expertise lies in system-level design for electro-mechanical/opto-mechanical products, with deep knowledge in GD&T, thermal engineering, kinematics and dynamics in product design & development.

With extensive experience in linear, non-linear, implicit and explicit Finite Element analysis, I specialize in performing validation and test experiments for post-processing and statistical analysis. I support product roadmaps by researching new sensor technologies and developing comprehensive reports on feasibility analysis to communicate simulation results, insights, and recommendations to both internal teams and external suppliers.
